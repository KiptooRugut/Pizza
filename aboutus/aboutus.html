ABOUT US

Pizza House is a unique place. It attracts with its original interior but above all the quality of the dishes served. Carefully prepared dishes are characterized by freshness and a lot of ingredients. Savor our dishes in the elegant and discreet atmosphere of our restaurant.

WE USE FRESH INGREDIENTS. WHY IS THAT?

Using fresh ingredients on our pizza not only means it's tastier, but it's also healthier. Taste is the first and foremost reason for using fresh ingredients. Using fresh ingredients will give our customers the chance to indulge in their favorite food without having to eat empty calories that degrade their health.
